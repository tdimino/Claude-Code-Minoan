# Emoji Style Examples Guide

Visual guide to the available transformation styles for photo-to-emoji conversion.

## Style Comparison

| Style | Best For | File Size | Complexity | Description |
|-------|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **classic_emoji** | Faces, expressions | Medium | Medium | Round, vibrant, Apple/Unicode style |
| **flat_icon** | Objects, logos | Small | Low | Minimalist, geometric, Material Design |
| **kawaii_sticker** | Animals, characters | Medium | Medium | Cute, playful, large eyes |
| **pixel_art** | Icons, retro | Very Small | Low | 8-bit, chunky pixels, limited colors |
| **chibi** | People, characters | Medium | High | Anime super-deformed, oversized head |
| **cartoon** | Dynamic subjects | Medium | High | Exaggerated features, bold outlines |
| **line_art** | Simple subjects | Very Small | Low | Black lines only, no color |
| **sticker_pack** | Messaging | Medium | Medium | Bold, vibrant, slight shadow |
| **memoji** | Portraits | Large | High | 3D-rendered, soft shading |
| **retro_emoji** | Smiley faces | Small | Low | Vintage yellow circle, 1970s style |

## Detailed Style Descriptions

### 1. Classic Emoji üòä

**Aesthetic**: Round, simplified features, expressive, warm colors
**Inspiration**: Apple/Unicode emoji standard
**Best use cases**: Facial expressions, animals, reactions

**Characteristics**:
- Round or circular shape
- Simplified, iconic features
- Bold black outlines
- Vibrant yellow/warm color palette
- Minimal shading (flat or subtle gradients)
- Expressive eyes and mouth
- White or transparent background
- Instantly recognizable at small sizes

**Example prompts**:
- "happy cat face" ‚Üí Round yellow cat with big smile
- "surprised person" ‚Üí Yellow face with wide eyes and open mouth
- "dog with tongue out" ‚Üí Friendly brown dog face, tongue visible

**File size**: Typically 40-55 KB (within Slack limits)

---

### 2. Flat Icon üéØ

**Aesthetic**: Minimalist, geometric, modern flat design
**Inspiration**: Material Design, iOS icons, modern UI
**Best use cases**: Objects, tools, symbols, logos

**Characteristics**:
- Simple geometric shapes (circles, squares, triangles)
- Solid colors, no gradients
- Bold, clean outlines
- Minimal detail
- Modern, professional appearance
- White background
- Focus on essential form

**Example prompts**:
- "coffee cup" ‚Üí Simple circular cup, solid brown, steam lines
- "camera icon" ‚Üí Rectangular body, circular lens
- "house symbol" ‚Üí Triangular roof, square base, door

**File size**: Typically 25-40 KB (very efficient)

---

### 3. Kawaii Sticker üêæ

**Aesthetic**: Cute, playful, Japanese kawaii culture
**Inspiration**: LINE stickers, Japanese character design
**Best use cases**: Animals, food, characters, cute subjects

**Characteristics**:
- Rounded, soft features
- Large, expressive eyes (sparkles optional)
- Small mouth (often "uwu" or ":3" expression)
- Pastel or vibrant colors
- Simple cel-shading
- Bold outlines
- Adorable, friendly vibe
- White background

**Example prompts**:
- "cute bunny" ‚Üí Round bunny with huge eyes, small nose
- "happy pizza slice" ‚Üí Smiling pizza with kawaii face
- "blushing cat" ‚Üí Cat with pink cheeks, big eyes

**File size**: Typically 45-60 KB (more colors = larger)

---

### 4. Pixel Art üéÆ

**Aesthetic**: Retro 8-bit/16-bit gaming
**Inspiration**: Classic Nintendo, Sega, arcade games
**Best use cases**: Icons, retro themes, small details

**Characteristics**:
- Chunky, visible pixels (32x32 or similar)
- Limited color palette (8-16 colors)
- Clean pixel outlines
- Simple shading (dithering optional)
- Nostalgic gaming aesthetic
- Clear subject definition
- Transparent or solid background

**Example prompts**:
- "sword icon" ‚Üí 8-bit sword with metallic gray pixels
- "heart" ‚Üí Red pixelated heart, black outline
- "treasure chest" ‚Üí Brown chest with gold lock

**File size**: Typically 15-30 KB (very compact)

---

### 5. Chibi Anime üå∏

**Aesthetic**: Super-deformed anime/manga style
**Inspiration**: Japanese anime, manga, character design
**Best use cases**: People, anime characters, personified animals

**Characteristics**:
- Oversized head (1/3 of total height)
- Small, cute body
- Large, expressive anime eyes
- Simplified facial features
- Bold black outlines
- Vibrant anime colors
- Exaggerated proportions
- White background

**Example prompts**:
- "person with glasses" ‚Üí Chibi character, large head, tiny body
- "warrior character" ‚Üí Cute warrior, oversized sword
- "business person" ‚Üí Chibi in suit, briefcase

**File size**: Typically 45-55 KB

---

- Cel-shaded coloring
- Dynamic, energetic appearance
- Vibrant, saturated colors
- Simplified details
- White background

**Example prompts**:
- "excited dog" ‚Üí Jumping dog, exaggerated expression
- "laughing person" ‚Üí Wide smile, squinting eyes

---

## File Size Optimization Tips

### If Your Emoji Exceeds 64KB:

1. **Choose a simpler style**:
   - line_art, pixel_art, flat_icon compress better
   - Avoid memoji, kawaii_sticker (use more colors)

2. **Simplify the description**:
   - "happy cat" instead of "happy fluffy Persian cat with blue eyes"
   - Less detail = simpler image = smaller file

3. **Use aggressive optimization**:
   ```python
   from core.optimizer import aggressive_optimize
   aggressive_optimize("emoji.png", "emoji_optimized.png", target_size_kb=60)
   ```

4. **Reduce color palette**:
   ```python
   convert_photo_to_emoji(..., max_colors=32)  # Instead of 48
   ```

## Custom Style Prompts

You can create your own custom styles:

```python
convert_photo_to_emoji(
    input_path="photo.jpg",
    output_path="emoji.png",
    style="custom",
    custom_prompt="""
    Transform this into a watercolor painting style:
    soft edges, pastel colors, gentle brushstrokes,
    artistic and hand-painted appearance,
    white background with subtle texture.
    """
)
```

### Custom Style Ideas:
- **Neon glow**: Bright neon colors, glowing edges, dark background
- **Sketch**: Pencil drawing, hand-drawn lines, grayscale
- **Stained glass**: Bold outlines, colorful sections, mosaic-like
- **Paper cut**: Layered paper aesthetic, shadows, depth
- **Graffiti**: Street art style, spray paint texture, bold
- **Vintage poster**: Retro colors, halftone dots, aged look

## Style Mixing

While you can't directly mix styles, you can add style modifiers to prompts:

```python
# Kawaii + flat design
"kawaii-style flat icon: cute but minimal, simple shapes, large eyes, solid colors"

# Cartoon + pixel art
"cartoon in pixel art style: exaggerated features but chunky pixels, 8-bit colors"

# Classic emoji + neon
"classic emoji with neon glow: round face, simplified, glowing edges, vibrant neon colors"
```

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with classic_emoji** - Most reliable, best compression
2. **Use descriptive subjects** - "smiling golden retriever" better than just "dog"
3. **Test at 16px size** - If it looks good small, it's perfect
4. **Simple backgrounds work best** - Gemini handles subject isolation well
5. **Iterate with custom prompts** - Refine until you get exactly what you want

## Troubleshooting Style Results

### Problem: Too much detail
**Solution**: Use flat_icon, line_art, or pixel_art

### Problem: Colors look washed out
**Solution**: Try kawaii_sticker or cartoon for more vibrant colors

### Problem: Subject not simplified enough
**Solution**: Add "extremely simplified" or "minimal detail" to description

### Problem: Background not clean
**Solution**: Add "pure white background" or "transparent background" to prompt

### Problem: Doesn't look like an emoji
**Solution**: Stick to classic_emoji or retro_emoji styles
