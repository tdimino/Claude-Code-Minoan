# Directors and Themes Reference

Curated lists of directors and thematic categories aligned with the Crypt Librarian sensibility.

## Directors by Sensibility

### The Ritualists (Occult/Sacred)
- **Stanley Kubrick** — Eyes Wide Shut, The Shining, Barry Lyndon
- **Nicolas Roeg** — Don't Look Now, The Man Who Fell to Earth
- **Ken Russell** — The Devils, Altered States, Gothic
- **Roman Polanski** — Rosemary's Baby, The Ninth Gate, The Tenant
- **Dario Argento** (pre-gore work) — Suspiria (1977), Inferno
- **Robin Hardy** — The Wicker Man (1973)

### The Gothic Romantics
- **Neil Jordan** — Interview with the Vampire, The Company of Wolves, Byzantium, In Dreams
- **Francis Ford Coppola** — Bram Stoker's Dracula
- **Guillermo del Toro** (pre-2016) — Crimson Peak, Pan's Labyrinth, The Devil's Backbone
- **Tim Burton** (early work) — Sleepy Hollow, Edward Scissorhands
- **Jean Cocteau** — Beauty and the Beast (1946), Orpheus

### The Literary Adapters
- **Pier Paolo Pasolini** — The Decameron, Canterbury Tales, Arabian Nights, Medea
- **James Ivory / Merchant Ivory** — A Room with a View, Howards End, The Remains of the Day
- **Peter Greenaway** — The Pillow Book, Prospero's Books, The Cook The Thief His Wife & Her Lover
- **Franco Zeffirelli** — Romeo and Juliet (1968), Hamlet (1990)
- **John Huston** — The Dead, Under the Volcano, The Man Who Would Be King

### The Epic Visionaries
- **Ridley Scott** — Blade Runner, Gladiator, Kingdom of Heaven (Director's Cut)
- **Oliver Stone** — Alexander (Director's Cut), JFK, The Doors
- **David Lean** — Lawrence of Arabia, Doctor Zhivago, A Passage to India
- **Bernardo Bertolucci** — The Last Emperor, 1900, The Sheltering Sky
- **Terrence Malick** — The Thin Red Line, The New World, Days of Heaven

### The Noir Revisionists
- **Robert Altman** — The Long Goodbye, Thieves Like Us
- **Roman Polanski** — Chinatown
- **Arthur Penn** — Night Moves
- **Alan J. Pakula** — Klute, The Parallax View, All the President's Men
- **Coen Brothers** (pre-2016) — Blood Simple, Miller's Crossing, The Man Who Wasn't There

### The Mystical/Transcendent
- **Andrei Tarkovsky** — Stalker, Nostalghia, The Sacrifice, Andrei Rublev
- **Carl Theodor Dreyer** — Vampyr, Ordet, Day of Wrath
- **Ingmar Bergman** — The Seventh Seal, Persona, Fanny and Alexander
- **Werner Herzog** — Nosferatu the Vampyre, Aguirre the Wrath of God, Fitzcarraldo
- **Lars von Trier** (early work) — Europa, The Kingdom

### The Sensual Classicists
- **Luchino Visconti** — Death in Venice, The Leopard, Senso
- **Bernardo Bertolucci** — The Conformist, Last Tango in Paris
- **Louis Malle** — Pretty Baby (with caution), Damage, Murmur of the Heart
- **Philip Kaufman** — The Unbearable Lightness of Being, Henry & June

## Director's Cut Preferences

These films are significantly improved in extended versions:
- **Alexander** — Theatrical is compromised; Director's Cut or Final Cut essential
- **Kingdom of Heaven** — Director's Cut transforms the film
- **Blade Runner** — Final Cut preferred
- **The Exorcist** — Extended Director's Cut
- **Apocalypse Now** — Redux or Final Cut
